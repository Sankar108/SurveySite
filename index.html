Product Survey

Are you launching a new product ?

We are there for you to provide a platform to carry out an extensive survey beyond your geographical limits and boundaries. Receive feedback from the audience, analyze the demand, and know their pulse with the data gathered from the research. These Surveys can be utilized to elevate customer satisfaction and improve the overall experience.

The sample templates for the survey are created by experts in corresponding fields of product research, valuations, customer feedback, and testing. These templates can also be customized as per your requirements.

Take a quick survey

HR Survey

Are your employees satisfied with the company policies ?

Employer and Employee coordination is an obligation for a successful organization to build a better workplace and environment. The HR (Human Resources) Survey is carried out to solicit employee opinions on a range of issues like the company’s success in communicating mission to its employees or local issues like quality of the work environment.

The Employee opinion surveys aid the employer to identify areas of employee satisfaction and dissatisfaction. The Survey helps the company in modifying their existing policies and benefits they provide to the employees.

Industrial Survey

Does your company want to track industry trends ?

Industry survey is a valuable tool for any enterprise to survey a particular field. These help to gain insight into a particular field, improve your product to meet consumers' needs along with identification of evolving trends. This involves a field survey where data based on statistics on financial activities in the industrial domain are gathered from a sample of selected industrial establishments.

Does your company want to get feedback on an innovative idea ?

The survey includes on-shore and offshore surveys for the different types of industries.

Customer Satisfaction Survey

Are your customers satisfied with your product/services ?

The customer satisfaction survey is the answer to this. This survey provides a set of questionnaires especially curated to help businesses understand what their consumers think about products/services, type of customer support, and their brand.

What are the parameters you can improve upon ?

It helps companies to know their customer’s views and opinions, their experiences with the products and services along with giving them an advantage of choosing their survey location from a remote location.

After sales service survey

Are you getting the right after-sales service ?

In the service sector industry, after the delivery of a service/product, the companies make sure that their customers are provided with after-sales services. This survey helps the firms to make sure their consumers are getting the right type of services in the automobile, banking, airline, hospital industry, etc. with no location barrier.

Property Survey

Need help in looking for the right land/property around You ?

The property survey involves legal research about a property along with field surveying, mapping, and preparing a report for the same. A property survey is carried out before the start of a construction project, purchasing a property, or financing a home.

The due diligence is done to pick the best surveyors, attend surveys and pick the best of the follow-up questions.

Environmental and Forest Survey

Need help in looking for the right land/property around You ?

A Forest survey is carried out for study and research to monitor the changing situation of forest and land resources. This data is used by the agencies responsible for sustainable development and environmental protection.

The environmental survey helps in understanding the concern level citizens have for the problems like pollution, hazardous substances, and change in climatic conditions. Both environmental and forest surveys cover the topics related to wildlife and land in the forests, flora, and fauna, tree census, poaching of animals and forest-related incidents, environmental-related concerns.

Agriculture Survey

How do the crops reach the consumers ? What is the crop growth ratio in a particular land ?

A Forest survey is carried out for study and research to monitor the changing situation of forest and land resources. This data is used by the agencies responsible for sustainable development and environmental protection.

The agriculture survey has got all the answers. Various agencies are involved in the process of performing the supply chain survey of the crops and their insurance policies. This helps in analyzing the loopholes in the supply chain process and improve upon the same.

Also, a survey is carried out for the crop insurance in case the crop fields are damaged by some calamity.

Geology and Mining Survey

Need to survey for a particular mine or borehole ?

The geological survey involves collecting, archiving, processing, and providing geoscientific information to the government/private/commercial clients. The mining Surveys are used to find out potential mining locations, gather information about mine and tunnel dimensions.

Borehole surveying is a major part of the geological survey that involves the procedure of determining the target point and course by a borehole used to record the information thus obtained.

Archaeological survey

Often wondered from where does all the statistics and information about a heritage site is obtained ?

In the field of archaeology, a field survey is a kind of research through with archeologists collect data about an archaeological site’s location, organization of human culture, and the distribution in terms of area and culture. This plays a vital role in preserving the heritage of a place. This involves collecting accurate data of a location and site mapping to gather every detail and then perform research on the same.

Health Survey

Are you living in a safe place ?

A Health survey normally involves a measure of risk factors, non-health determinants, and health behaviors. This includes a survey of hospitals, a survey with patients, and a survey about the hospital facilities and amenities available for the patients.

In the current pandemic situation, quarantine facilities need to be surveyed along with surveying areas on certain parameters such as population, spread rate, etc. for setting up of new ones.

Home and security survey

A home security survey is conducted to identify the theft and crime vulnerability of any area. This also helps in determining the level of awareness among the public, their concerns, the security measures taken for their safety, and the satisfaction level among the people. The surveys also identify the security device purchasing habits among the people depending on the area of living.

The survey also covers topics like a crime scene and accident mapping for investigation, hotspot analysis, and study and performing surveys by crime locations.

Utility Survey

A utility survey is generally performed to locate the underground wires and pipelines to locate gas, power, water, fiber optics, drainage, telecommunications, and sewage so that these services run uninterrupted during any excavation work or building redevelopment work.

This involves careful observation of utilities and the structure of the network installed for their transportation of those particular utilities in an area. This process helps to add assets to the utility network and their uninterrupted operation.

Land Survey

Land survey permits understanding the land boundaries. This survey is done to locate and map the corners and boundaries of a land parcel. It can also include the topography of the land, the location of buildings, and other relevant factors. The Survey integrates with DGPS giving improved location accuracy with the help of equipment like total station etc.

Location plays an important role in land surveys and one can survey a remote location.
